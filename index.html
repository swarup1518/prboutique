How We Style You

From understanding your preferences to delivering your final look — here’s how our styling process works at PR Boutique.

Understanding Your Style We begin by learning your preferences, occasion & comfort to understand what suits you
Curating Outfit Options We shortlist outfits, fabrics, and combinations that match your personality and desired look.
Try-On & Styling You try the selected pieces while we guide you with styling, accessories, and finishing touches.
Alterations & Fit Check we refine the fit with professional alterations for a polished and perfect final look.
Final Selection You confirm your chosen outfit and we make any adjustments to ensure everything feels right.
Show Your Best Self Your complete look is ready — designed to make you feel confident, stylish, and uniquely you.
